London History

• London is an old city with many historic sites.The Romans founded London in the 1st century A.D. The city was part of the Roman Empire for about four hundred years.Later, the Saxons, a Germanic people, settled there. William the Conqueror, coming from Normandy, France, took over England in 1066. He had a fortress built - later called the Tower of London - in the city.

• London grew rapidly. Several times, however, an epidemic disease called the black plague has killed many people. In 1666, a fire destroyed the city, but it was rebuilt

• Over the years, the English built a great empire, and London grew and became rich as its capital. The city was the center of the world economy from the end of the 18th century to the beginning of the 20th
