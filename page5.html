British Royal Family

There is no way to not mention the british Royal Family when it come to United Kingdom, right ? So let's learn a little bit about this big and important family.

- The British royal family is the family that governs the United Kingdom (England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland) together with the English parliament.

- Members of the royal family have, by birth or marriage, been in the House of Windsor since 1917.

- Although there is no strictly legal or formal definition in the UK for who is and is not a member of the royal family, the term His Majesty (SM) or His Royal Highness (SAR) is generally used to treat family members, which generally results in the application of the term for monarchs, the sons of the monarch, the grandchildren of the monarchs, widows and for all ancestors of current or former monarchs
