Hello, my name is Larissa and today I will be taking you on a journey to United Kingdom!!

• Let's start with the famous UK flag and understand a little about the meaning behind it.

• There is a lot of meaning behind the UK flag.The UK flag can also be called the Union flag. Since it represents the unity formed by the countries that make up the United Kingdom - England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.

• The first cross represented is the Saint George, which represents England.

• The second cross represented is the Saint Andrew, which represents Scotland.

• The third cross represented is the Saint Patrick, which represents Ireland

• Wales does not have your own cross represented so it's being represented by the cross of Saint George as well.
